Purriod Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Purriod (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Android app, Purriod.

2. Data We Collect

Purriod only requests access to the following:

  • Camera – to let you take photos of your cat within the app.
  • Local storage – to save the photos you take in the app’s private storage area.

We do not collect or transmit any other personal data, including location, contacts, or identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • App functionality: Camera and storage access are solely used to capture and save photos locally.
  • No sharing: Your photos and any metadata remain on your device. We do not upload or share them with any third party.

4. Data Security

All photos and data are stored locally on your device in the app’s private storage space. We do not send any data over the network. You are solely responsible for any backup you choose to make.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your saved photos at any time from within the app:

  1. Open Purriod and go to Settings.
  2. Select Clear All Photos.
  3. Confirm deletion.

This permanently removes all images from your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

Purriod is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the new effective date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please email us at avalin@example.com.

Thank you for using Purriod!
