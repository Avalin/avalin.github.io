Purriod Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Purriod (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Android app, Purriod.

2. Data We Collect

Purriod requests access to the following:

  • Camera – to let you take photos of your cat within the app.
  • Local storage – to save the photos you take in the app’s private storage area.

Purriod does not collect personal data such as your name, email address, contacts, or location. Your photos and cat data remain stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • App functionality: Camera and storage access are solely used to capture and save photos locally.
  • No sharing: Your photos and any metadata remain on your device and are not shared with third parties.

4. Analytics

Purriod uses analytics services to help improve the app’s functionality, stability, and user experience.

These services may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App usage statistics (such as which features are used)
  • Crash reports and performance data

This information is collected in aggregated and anonymous form and is used solely to improve the app.

Purriod may use analytics services provided by third parties such as Google. These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

Photos and cat-related data are stored locally on your device in the app’s private storage space. Purriod does not upload or store your personal content on external servers.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your saved photos and related data at any time from within the app:

  1. Open Purriod and go to Settings.
  2. Select Clear All Photos.
  3. Confirm deletion.

This permanently removes the images from your device.

7. Children’s Privacy

Purriod is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the new effective date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please email us at avalin@example.com.

Thank you for using Purriod!
